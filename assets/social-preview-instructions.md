# Social Preview Upload

Your social preview image is at `assets/social-preview.svg`.

To set it on GitHub:
1. Go to https://github.com/Rishiidev/seed-discussions/settings
2. Scroll to "Social preview"
3. Click **Edit** → upload `assets/social-preview.svg`
4. Click **Save**

Size: 1280×640px ✓

This image appears when someone shares your repo link on Twitter, LinkedIn, Slack, or iMessage.
